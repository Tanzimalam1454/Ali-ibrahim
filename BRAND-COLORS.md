## Color Contrast Ratios

All color combinations meet **WCAG AA accessibility standards**:

✓ Navy text on white background: **10.2:1** (Excellent)
✓ Red buttons with white text: **5.8:1** (Great)
✓ Light navy text on white: **5.1:1** (Pass)
