# 🎨 Add Your Logos - Simple Instructions

## 📂 Two Folders Created

### 1️⃣ Company Logo
```
📁 public/images/logo/
   └── logo.png  ← PUT YOUR COMPANY LOGO HERE
```

**What to do:**
- Save your company logo as `logo.png`
- Drop it in: `public/images/logo/`
- Refresh website ✓

---

### 2️⃣ Client Logos
```
📁 public/images/clients/
   ├── client-1.png  ← PUT CLIENT LOGOS HERE
   ├── client-2.png
   └── client-3.png
```

**What to do:**
- Save client logos as PNG files
- Drop them in: `public/images/clients/`
- Open `index.html` and update the Clients section (instructions inside)
- Refresh website ✓

---

## ✅ That's It!

Just add your images to these two folders:
- **Logo folder:** `public/images/logo/logo.png`
- **Clients folder:** `public/images/clients/*.png`

Then refresh your browser and they'll appear on the website!
