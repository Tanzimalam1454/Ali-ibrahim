# Images Needed for New Design

The new light, modern, photography-driven design requires the following images to be added to the `assets/` folder:

## Required Images

### 1. Hero Image
- **Filename:** `hero-construction.jpg`
- **Recommended size:** 1920x1080px minimum
- **Subject:** Wide shot of a construction site, modern building under construction, or construction equipment
- **Style:** Professional, well-lit, impressive scale
- **Example:** Aerial view of construction site, modern architecture being built, or large-scale construction operation

### 2. Service Images

#### Manpower Service
- **Filename:** `service-manpower.jpg`
- **Recommended size:** 800x600px minimum
- **Subject:** Construction workers, team in action, workers with equipment
- **Style:** Professional workers in safety gear, active work environment

#### Contracting Service
- **Filename:** `service-contracting.jpg`
- **Recommended size:** 800x600px minimum
- **Subject:** Building construction, structural work, completed projects
- **Style:** Modern commercial or industrial building, construction in progress

#### Logistics Service
- **Filename:** `service-logistics.jpg`
- **Recommended size:** 800x600px minimum
- **Subject:** Construction vehicles, fleet of trucks, equipment transportation
- **Style:** Clean commercial vehicles, organized fleet, logistics operations

## Temporary Solution

Until you add your own photos, the design will show:
- A white overlay where images should be (images won't break the layout)
- All text and structure will display correctly
- The site will still look professional with placeholders

## Image Guidelines

For best results, use images that are:
- **High quality** - minimum 1200px wide for services, 1920px for hero
- **Well-lit** - bright, professional photography
- **On-brand** - show actual company operations if possible
- **Consistent style** - similar lighting and tone across all images
- **Optimized** - compressed for web (use WebP or JPEG, < 500KB each)
